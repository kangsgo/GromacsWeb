Skin

Customize your documentation template with variety of available skins.

How to

Simple add your desire skin style to header of page, right after main css file. For example to change skin to blue color, you should include the following file:

<link href="assets/css/skin-blue.css" rel="stylesheet">

Available skins

Check our available skins image and change the skin of this page just by clicking on "Set Skin" button.
